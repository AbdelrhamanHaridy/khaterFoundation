About us

Foundation Story

Our story started in the 90s, when philanthropist Dr. Salah Khater began supporting his community with donations to government and nongovernment healthcare entities that help underprivileged patients reach the healthcare services they need with the quality they deserve. Following in his father’s footsteps, Dr. Fahad Salah Khater continued fighting the good fight, and, since 2000, has been supporting countless local and international foundations and associations across Egypt, the United Kingdom, and the United States who help individuals in his community. His efforts have seen him become a board-member and active donor in a number of local foundations such as We Owe it to Egypt Foundation and Ahl Masr Foundation, providing both financial support and technical consultancy through Alameda Healthcare Group’s experts. In 2020, in order to institutionalize the extensive charity work of the Khater family and drive its sustainable growth, Dr. Fahad went on to establish the Khater Foundation for Community Development as a non-profit organization, with the core belief that an individual’s health and wellbeing is a fundamental right regardless of socio-economic, age, or ethnic background, and that an inclusively healthy environment contributes to a strong economy. The Foundation implements its vision, mission, and goals in full alignment with Egypt’s national Vision 2030 strategy, as well as the global vision of the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals1.

Approaches

Our Vision

To ensure equitable access to quality healthcare services, and to support individuals with the resources required to safeguard their well-being.

Our Mission

To safeguard the health and wellbeing of people living in Egypt, and tackle challenges that face the two ends of the healthcare spectrum by empowering both recipients and providers with the needed resources. The Khater Foundation works on ensuring accountability and sustainability while providing its medical and financial support to achieve its goals through three pillars: development; charity; and advocacy.

Our Goals

The Foundation’s goals dictate the projects it funds and the people it serves, and they are: Provide support to fill existing gaps and challenges faced in healthcare systems by advising on sustainable operations and availing solutions to beneficiaries Empower individuals to pursue their well-being Spread health and well-being awareness to help reshape the community

Who we Server

The Khater Foundation’s objective is to reach any marginalized community and anyone in need of medical healthcare services but cannot afford it, and invests its knowledge, resources, and efforts to elevate the health and well-being of all underprivileged individuals living in Egypt Egyptians, with the goal of raising the standards and improving the quality of services offered by healthcare providers, in order for Egyptians individuals to live a healthy life and contribute positively to their community and their economy.

Where we Serve

The Khater Foundation supports local communities and healthcare providers across the country. It began by focusing on Hihya City in Al Sharqia, one of Egypt’s largest governorates, and the hometown of the Khater family.
