About KhaterFoundation

Get to know about our KhaterFoundation organization

Our story started in the 90s, when philanthropist Dr. Salah Khater began supporting his community with donations to government and nongovernment healthcare entities that help underprivileged patients reach the healthcare services they need with the quality they deserve. Following in his father’s footsteps, Dr. Fahad Salah Khater continued fighting the good fight, and, since 2000, has been supporting countless local and international foundations and associations across Egypt, the United Kingdom, and the United States who help individuals in his community. His efforts have seen him become a board-member and active donor in a number of local foundations such as We Owe it to Egypt Foundation and Ahl Masr Foundation, providing both financial support and technical consultancy through Alameda Healthcare Group’s experts.

To date he has supported a myriad of healthcare entities, such as the National Cancer Institute, Suez Canal University Hospital, as well as Al Kasr Al Ainy, which Dr. Fahad considers a second home and to which he has provided various donations including medical supplies and equipment to the critical care unit “Prof. Dr Sherif Mokhtar's Unit”, Internal Medicine Hospital, and King Fahad’s Urology Unit. Dr. Fahad is also an active supporter of various local foundations and associations like Children’s Cancer Hospital Foundation “57357”, Magdi Yacoub Global Heart Foundation in Aswan, Face for Children in Need Foundation, and the Special Needs and Autism School in New Cairo. In 2020, in order to institutionalize the extensive charity work of the Khater family and drive its sustainable growth, Dr. Fahad went on to establish the Khater Foundation for Community Development as a non-profit organization, with the core belief that an individual’s health and wellbeing is a fundamental right regardless of socio-economic, age, or ethnic background, and that an inclusively healthy environment contributes to a strong economy. The Foundation implements its vision, mission, and goals in full alignment with Egypt’s national Vision 2030 strategy, as well as the global vision of the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals.
