One of the Khater Foundation’s very first projects is in Hihya, Al Sharqia, in collaboration with As-Salam International Hospital, which utilizes the hospital’s experienced physicians and nurses, as well as its reputable pharmacy. Convoys were launched across Al Sharqia between September 2018 and March 2020, with a short hiatus during the COVID-19 pandemic and is back to the community again in September 2022. The convoys provided patients with physician consultations and medication free of charge. The convoys offered weekly clinics covering three specialties (General Medicine – Paediatrics – Ear, Nose, & Throat), in addition to occasional clinics for General Surgery and Ophthalmology.
